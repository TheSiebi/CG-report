Advanced Computer Graphics Project Report

  1. Michael Siebenmann & Marc Matter

This is the project report for the Advanced Computer Graphics course at ETH Zurich in the fall semester 2023.

As a motivational image, we chose the famous "Relativity" by MC Escher (note that the original version is black and white). This image conforms to the theme "The more you look", as one has to surely look at the image multiple times to understand what is going on, namely that there is gravity in three directions. Furthermore, there are various details that one notices while the time looked at the image increases.
